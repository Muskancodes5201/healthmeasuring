Morning Brisk Walk
Cardio Beginner
▼
30 min
Duration
200 kcal
Burns
Daily
Frequency

A 30-minute brisk walk every morning is the most effective low-impact exercise. It raises your heart rate, improves circulation, and sets a positive tone for the day.

📋 HOW TO DO IT
  • Wear comfortable shoes and breathable clothes.
  • Start with 5-min slow warm-up walk.
  • Increase pace — slightly out of breath but can still talk.
  • Swing arms naturally, keep upright posture.
  • End with 5-min cool-down and light leg stretches.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
❤️Lowers blood pressure — reduces heart disease risk by 30%
🩸Controls blood sugar — great for Type 2 diabetes
🧠Releases endorphins — reduces anxiety and depression
⚖️Helps maintain healthy weight and metabolism
😴Improves sleep quality at night

Surya Namaskar
Flexibility Strength Beginner
▼
20 min
Duration
150 kcal
Burns
Daily
Frequency

Surya Namaskar is a complete body workout in 12 steps. It combines stretching, breathing, and strength — great for flexibility and mental calm. Do 5–12 rounds each morning.

📋 12 POSES SEQUENCE
  • Pranamasana — Stand, palms together. Inhale.
  • Hasta Uttanasana — Arms up and back. Inhale.
  • Hasta Padasana — Forward bend, touch toes. Exhale.
  • Ashwa Sanchalanasana — Lunge back, look up. Inhale.
  • Dandasana — Plank position. Hold.
  • Ashtanga Namaskara — Knees, chest, forehead down. Exhale.
  • Bhujangasana — Cobra pose, arch back. Inhale.
  • Adho Mukha — Downward dog. Exhale.
  • Lunge forward again. Inhale.
  • Forward bend again. Exhale.
  • Arms up, stretch. Inhale.
  • Return to prayer pose. Exhale.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🌿Full body stretch — spine, hips, shoulders flexibility
💪Tones arms, core, thighs, and back
🧠Calms mind, reduces cortisol (stress hormone)
🌬️Deep breathing improves lung capacity
⚡Boosts energy and metabolism all day

Squats are the king of lower body exercises. They strengthen quads, glutes, hamstrings, and core without any equipment. Perfect to do at home anytime.

📋 HOW TO DO IT
  • Stand feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out.
  • Keep chest up, back straight, core tight.
  • Push hips back, bend knees until thighs are parallel to floor.
  • Knees track over toes — don't collapse inward.
  • Drive through heels to stand back up. That's 1 rep.
  • Rest 60 sec between sets.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🦵Builds and tones legs — quads, glutes, hamstrings
🔥Burns 300–400 kcal per full session
🦴Improves bone density and joint health
⚖️Boosts metabolism even at rest
💪Engages core — builds abdominal strength

Pranayama (Breathing)

▼
15 min
Duration
50 kcal
Burns
Daily

Pranayama (controlled breathing) is the most powerful practice for heart, lungs, and mental health. Do it in the morning on an empty stomach for best results.

📋 KEY TECHNIQUES
  • Anulom Vilom: Close right nostril, inhale left. Close left, exhale right. 10 rounds.
  • Bhramari: Hum like a bee on exhale, thumbs on ears. 5 rounds.
  • Kapalbhati: Quick forceful exhales, 1/sec, for 2 min.
  • 4-7-8: Inhale 4 sec, hold 7 sec, exhale 8 sec. Deeply calming.
  • Sit cross-legged, spine straight throughout.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🫁Increases lung capacity by up to 50%
❤️Lowers heart rate and blood pressure
🧠Reduces anxiety and panic attacks
😴4-7-8 breathing cures insomnia
⚡Increases energy and mental clarity

The plank activates your entire core, back, and stabilizer muscles simultaneously. It's the single best exercise to build a strong, pain-free back.

📋 HOW TO DO IT
  • Forearms on ground, elbows under shoulders.
  • Body in straight line from head to heels — no sagging.
  • Pull belly button toward spine to engage core.
  • Breathe normally. Hold 20–60 seconds.
  • Rest 30 sec between sets. Do 3 sets daily.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🦴Eliminates lower back pain permanently
💪Activates all core muscles: abs, obliques, back
🏃Improves posture and reduces neck strain
⚖️Improves balance and body coordination

Jumping jacks are a classic full-body cardio exercise done anywhere. Great for warming up, burning calories fast, and improving cardiovascular fitness quickly.

📋 HOW TO DO IT
  • Stand straight, feet together, arms at sides.
  • Jump, spread feet wide, raise arms overhead.
  • Jump back to starting position.
  • Land softly on balls of feet.
  • Maintain steady rhythm; increase speed gradually.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🔥Burns 100 calories in just 10 minutes
❤️Excellent cardio — raises heart rate fast
🦴Improves bone density
😊Instant mood boost from endorphins

10 min
Duration
20 kcal

Daily

Restorative yoga poses for recovery, relaxation, and nervous system reset. Perfect before bed or after intense workouts to reduce soreness and stress.

📋 HOW TO DO IT
  • Balasana: Kneel, sit on heels, extend arms forward, forehead down. Hold 2 min.
  • Feel stretch in lower back, hips, thighs. Breathe deeply.
  • Shavasana: Lie flat on back, arms at sides (palms up), eyes closed.
  • Relax every muscle from toes to face.
  • Stay 5–10 minutes. Focus only on breath.
✅ HEALTH BENEFITS
🧠Deep calm — activates parasympathetic nervous system
🦴Relieves lower back, hip, and knee tension
😴Dramatically improves sleep quality
🩸Lowers cortisol and blood pressure within minutes
🌅
Ideal Daily Routine: Morning — Surya Namaskar + Pranayama (30 min). Afternoon — 30-min walk. Evening — Squats + Plank (20 min). Bedtime — Balasana + Shavasana (10 min).
